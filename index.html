About the Doctor

A lifetime of precision in ophthalmology

Dr. Mahmoud Sami is a distinguished Consultant Ophthalmologist based in Cairo, with advanced postgraduate qualifications from leading surgical bodies. His practice spans the full spectrum of ophthalmic surgery — from restoring sight through cataract correction to transforming lives with refractive and oculoplastic procedures.

With a philosophy centred on patient trust and surgical precision, Dr. Sami brings a meticulous, evidence-based approach to every consultation and procedure — ensuring optimal outcomes with the utmost care.

MSc Master of Science in Ophthalmology — advanced academic training in eye sciences
MRCSEd Member of the Royal College of Surgeons of Edinburgh — internationally recognised surgical qualification
FICO Fellow of the International Council of Ophthalmology — global standard of ophthalmic excellence

Surgical Specialisations

Expert care across every dimension of eye health

Cataract Surgery

State-of-the-art phacoemulsification and premium IOL implantation to restore clear, crisp vision.

Refractive Surgery

Laser vision correction and lens-based refractive procedures for freedom from glasses and contact lenses.

Lid Surgery

Precise eyelid reconstruction and cosmetic procedures addressing ptosis, ectropion, entropion, and more.

Lacrimal Surgery

Dacryocystorhinostomy (DCR) and lacrimal drainage procedures to treat chronic tearing and infections.

Plastic & Orbital

Oculoplastic procedures including orbital reconstruction and periocular aesthetic surgery with exceptional precision.

Comprehensive Eye Care

Full ophthalmic examinations, medical management, and long-term eye health monitoring for all ages.
